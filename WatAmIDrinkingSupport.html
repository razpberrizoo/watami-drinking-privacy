About the App

WatAmI Drinking helps you remember what you drank, where you had it, and what you thought — without spreadsheets or complexity.

## FAQs

**How do I scan a bottle?** Open the app, tap Scan, and point your camera at the bottle label.

**Can I edit or delete entries?** Yes. Any drink you add can be edited or removed at any time.

**Is my data backed up?** All data is stored locally on your device. If you remove the app, your data will be removed as well.
