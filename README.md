# watami-drinking-privacy
Last updated: January 2026

WatAmI Drinking is designed for personal use. This privacy policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

⸻

Information You Provide

WatAmI Drinking allows you to save information such as:
	•	Drinks you scan or add
	•	Ratings and personal notes
	•	Where or when you tasted a drink

This information is entered voluntarily by you.

⸻

How Information Is Used

Information saved in the app is used only to support its core features, such as:
	•	Identifying bottles
	•	Storing your personal drink history
	•	Displaying your notes, ratings, and collection

The app does not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

⸻

Data Storage

All information you save in WatAmI Drinking is stored locally on your device.

The app does not upload your personal drink data to external servers.

⸻

Data Sharing

WatAmI Drinking does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

⸻

Analytics and Tracking

WatAmI Drinking does not use third-party advertising trackers.

Basic system information may be processed by Apple as part of standard App Store and operating system functionality.

⸻

Children’s Privacy

WatAmI Drinking is not intended for use by children.

⸻

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
